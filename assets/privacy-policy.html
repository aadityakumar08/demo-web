Privacy Policy

SmartShop respects your privacy. This app uses your device's camera for barcode scanning, Bluetooth for printing, and internet for syncing products. No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.

What We Collect

  • Camera: Used only for scanning barcodes/QR codes.
  • Bluetooth: Used only to connect to your selected printer.
  • Internet: Used to sync product data with Google Sheets.

Data Storage

All scanned and cart data is stored locally on your device and is not shared.
